ETHL - Edinburgh Theoretical Historical Linguistics

Workshop

About

Hover over me! Hello World!

ETHL is a venue for postgraduate students and early career researchers in Historical Linguistics to consider the role of theory in Historical Linguistics as well as the place of Historical Linguistics within the wider field. We aim to give young researchers a platform to discuss ontological issues of our disciplines and provide the theoretical tools to perform this task. With linguistic departments under threat and funding bodies trending towards impact output, we think it is vital to carve out a space for the discussion of less flashy areas of research and to make opportunities for young academics, the future leaders of the field, to shape the future of Historical Linguistics.
